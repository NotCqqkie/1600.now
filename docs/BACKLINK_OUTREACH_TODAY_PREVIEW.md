## 1. Fairfax County Public Library

To: wwwlib@fairfaxcounty.gov

Subject: A free Digital SAT resource for your Test Preparation guide

Hi,

I'm a high school senior, and I built a free SAT prep site called 1600.now. I came across your Homework Help: Test Preparation guide while looking for places that point students to good free resources, and I was hoping you might consider adding it.

It's free, there are no ads, and students can use everything without making an account. There are around 8,000 questions for the digital SAT, each with a real explanation, plus timed practice modules, a score calculator, and vocab. I built it because solid prep usually costs money or caps how much you can practice, and I wanted something students could just open and use. Since nobody signs in, there's nothing to collect for library use, and the privacy details (COPPA/FERPA) are here if it ever matters: https://1600.now/privacy

If it looks like a fit, it'd mean a lot to have it on the guide:
https://research.fairfaxcounty.gov/homework/test-prep

And if not, no worries at all, thanks for reading.

Luke Finigan
